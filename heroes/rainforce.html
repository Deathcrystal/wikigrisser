Rainforce

Talent: Otherworldly Force

Each time you deal damage to the enemy, gain Otherworldly Force: All stats except for HP+(1, 2, 3, 4)%. Can be stacked up to 7 times. After taking action, if you have 7 stacks of Otherworldly Force, you may act again. After taking this action, you are teleported to the nearest ally and all Otherworldly Force effects are removed from you.

Bond Requirement:

  • Glory: Level 5 Intimacy.
  • Light: Level 10 Intimacy + Clear Time Rift 3-6 (Normal) with this character.
  • Honor: Level 15 Intimacy + Upgrade to Otherworldly Prince.
  • Toughness: Level 23 Intimacy + Clear Gate 4 with Gizarof's help.
  • Strength: Level 25 Intimacy + Clear Gate 5 with Sigma's help.

Talent: Holy Protection

Whenever a unit dies, summon a [Wolf Soul] adjacent to your location. The [Wolf Soul] gains the stats of the deceased general but cannot exceed 150% of Aka's stats ((3,2,2,1) turn cooldown). When [Wolf Soul] dies, Aka gains (10,13,16,20)% of the [Wolf Soul]'s non-HP stats & restore HP to Aka equal to (10,13,16, 20)% of the [Wolf Soul]'s max HP.
